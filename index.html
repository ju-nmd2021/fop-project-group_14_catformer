Cats love mischief, this is known. In Catformer, your mission is to find a beautiful yet fragile vase and knock it down before your owner is coming back. But beware the obsticales on your journey (especially the scary cleaning monster) for they will only slow you down.

Goal: Find and destroy the vase before the time runs out. The faster you are, the more superior of a cat.

Contols

Left arrow: Move to the left
Right arrow: Move to the right
Space bar: Jump
